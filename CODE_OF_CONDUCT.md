# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, technically serious collaboration.

Participants should:

- discuss ideas and code directly and in good faith
- focus on correctness, clarity, and evidence
- avoid harassment, insults, intimidation, or exclusionary behavior
- accept feedback and disagreement without personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

- personal abuse or derogatory language
- harassment or stalking
- deliberate disruption of project discussion or review
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for conduct that undermines a safe and productive working environment.

Serious or repeated violations may lead to a permanent ban from project spaces.
